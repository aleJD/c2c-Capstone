Enter your grades below!

Enter your grade into the fields on the left below. You can add more with the "Add Field" button, or remove them with the "Remove Field" button. Submit when you want to find the average of your grades.

By default, weighting is turned off, so each grade is as important as each other. This is ideal if you are calculating something such as your semester, six weeks, or section grade. However, most classes use sections such as quizzes, homework, and daily work, which are all weighted differently from each other. You can enable weighting on the bottom right.
